Have you ever...

  1. 👉 Create a Single Page Application from scratch? Svelte is simple to get start.
  2. 👉 Migrate a legacy web application? Svelte is simple to integrate.
  3. 👉 As a backend developer who wants to try frontend nowadays? Svelte is simpler to play with than React/Vue.

《Simple Svelte》 will cover from product design to technology implementations, I will guide you thru how to think, how to design and how to build even how to operate. With this book, you will learn more than just svelte3 and technology things, but also product, marketing and human natures.

对于高度内聚的软件研发活动来说（比如全栈-fullstack，比如独立开发者，比如精英小团队）， HTMX提供了一种小众但全新的开发体验。尤其对于后端开发者以及MPA时代走过来的开发者，HTMX可以让你的传统艺能继续发扬光大。

《HTMX揭秘》尝试由浅入深、从理论到实践， 全方位地为大家展示和剖析HTMX的威力与魅力。 工欲善其事，必先利其器，HTMX就是一把认知门槛不高的利器， 帮助我们在打造受欢迎的Web产品之路上狂奔。
