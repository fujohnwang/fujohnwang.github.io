Privacy First

Thank you for choosing mkdown. We believe that your writing is personal. Our philosophy is simple: what you create on your device stays on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how mkdown collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

Data Collection & Storage

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

  • No Personal Information: We do not require you to register an account or provide any personal details to use the app.
  • Local Storage: All your markdown files are stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud Drive container. We do not have access to your files.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics SDKs that track your behavior across other apps and websites.

Permissions

The app may request access to certain system features to function properly:

  • Photo Library / Files: Only requested if you choose to import images into your markdown documents. This access is strictly local.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
